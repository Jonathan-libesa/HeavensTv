Why Become a Partner

Heavens TV Partners have decided to commit themselves to the cause of OpenHeavensministry which is to reach a generation without faith and hope, telling the sweet story of our Lord Jesus Christ and reaching out to a troubled world with His compassion and generosity.

As An Heavens TV Partner, You Will Receive:

  1. A personal partner number.
  2. News and updates from the Heavens TV Team.
  3. Notice of upcoming crusades and events
  4. A dedicated prayer and counseling telephone line.
